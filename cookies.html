What are cookies?
Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file isstored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third­party to recognize you and make yournext visit easier and the Service more useful to you. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies.
